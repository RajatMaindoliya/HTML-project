Thank You!

Helping you help others, Dec 1, 2020
<

In Collaboration with Minds Matter,together we have raised over 5000 euro.

Last month, We reached a large milestone. Walk for Hope partnered with Minds Matter in May 2019.

Since then, You're hard work and dedication have helped fund several youth groups across the country.

The Minds Matter help-line has also been able to take on new workers and expand their services,

Keep up the hard work, You are making a difference!
